Microsoft Is Teaching Your Plants To Talk Back

Gardeners have long claimed that talking to plants helps them grow-an idea backed up by science. But more recently, we're discovering that plants talk, too, reacting to their environment in a language of electrical impulses and chemicals. The question is whether there's a way that we-humans and plants-could talk to one another?
